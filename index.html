About BLESSEDSAM ELECTRICAL INSTALLATIONS

Your trusted partner in electrical solutions since 2008

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by master electrician John Excellence, Excellence Electricals has grown from a small local business to one of the region's most trusted electrical service providers. Our commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction has earned us thousands of satisfied customers.

We specialize in both residential and commercial electrical work, bringing decades of combined experience to every project. Our team of certified electricians stays current with the latest electrical codes and technologies.
