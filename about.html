Our Story

Founded in 2008 by master electrician John Excellence, BLESSEDSAM ELECTRICAL has grown from a small local business to one of the region's most trusted electrical and sound system service providers. Our commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction has earned us thousands of satisfied customers across Kenya.

We specialize in both residential and commercial electrical work, as well as professional sound system installations and maintenance. Our team brings decades of combined experience to every project, from complex electrical installations to custom audio solutions. Our certified electricians and audio technicians stay current with the latest electrical codes, technologies, and sound system innovations.
